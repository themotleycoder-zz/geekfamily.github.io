About

We are a family of makers who enjoy doing geeky, but epic projects. In our garage, our wild ideas come to life. We all come together to design, prototype, and build new enterprises. Despite our different passions, and backgrounds, we are proof that you can all have different interests and still be able to create something incredible together. Our hope is that you find this site, and these projects, interesting as well as just the little bit nerdy, and that it inspires you to beome a maker! Feel free to give us feed back on ways to make our projects even cooler using the contact form below.
